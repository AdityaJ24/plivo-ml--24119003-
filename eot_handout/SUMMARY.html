Validation Audit: Held-Out (OOF) vs. In-Sample Fit

Critical Audit Finding: Training vs. Evaluation Data

To avoid false confidence from model memorization, we separate Held-Out Out-of-Fold (OOF) metrics from In-Sample refit metrics:

  • GroupKFold Cross-Validation (Held-Out Turns): Evaluates performance on turns never seen during training (5 splits grouped strictly by turn_id). This reflects true generalization to unseen test turns.
  • In-Sample Benchmark: Scores the final model fitted on the complete dataset. The near-1.0 AUC (0.988 / 0.996) represents tree ensemble capacity on known data.

Causality Leak Audit

Code Audit Results: 100% Causality Guarantee

Every line of feature extraction in features_advanced.py was audited line-by-line:

  1. Strict Audio Slicing: Audio is sliced via audio_full = x[:int(pause_start * sr)] and x[start:end] where end = int(pause_start * sr). Indexing strictly stops at end - 1, meaning sample at pause_start and all future audio are never read.
  2. Zero Reference to pause_end: Ground truth pause duration (pause_end - pause_start) is never referenced in feature extraction or prediction code. It exists only in score.py for metric evaluation.
  3. No Silence Trimming Leak: Framing and window extraction operate backwards from pause_start without looking ahead.

Signal Extraction Architecture (48 Features)

  • Pitch ($F_0$) Trajectory & Slope: Autocorrelation pitch tracking over trailing windows. Falling pitch slope indicates statement completion ($p_{\text{eot}} \uparrow$), while flat/rising pitch indicates continuation holds.
  • Short-Time Energy Decay: RMS energy in dB over trailing 300ms, 750ms, and 1500ms windows, along with energy drop ratio.
  • Zero-Crossing Rate (ZCR) & Spectral Cues: Identifies unvoiced boundary fricatives (/s/, /f/, /th/), Spectral Centroid, Rolloff (85%), Spectral Flux, and 10 MFCC coefficients + stds.
  • Conversational Temporal Context: Elapsed turn speech time ($t = \text{pause\_start}$) and pause index.
